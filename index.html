About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is a lightweight yet powerful tool designed to help users identify potentially harmful phishing URLs before they become victims of cyber attacks.

Our system uses a combination of rule-based analysis and machine learning techniques to analyze URLs and determine their risk level with high accuracy.

Phishing attacks remain one of the most common cyber threats, with millions of people falling victim each year. By using PhishGuard, you add an extra layer of protection to your online activities.
